India sit pretty at the top of the ODI rankings while the team from the Caribbean languishes at number 7. India were victorious 3-2 over Australia in a run-extravaganza just prior to the West Indies tour. A change in leadership is most welcome, given the fact that Darren Sammy went wicketless in whites. Clive Lloyd accused the West Indies of suffering from a T20 hangover in the Test series. Shikhar Dhawan is not far behind and is just 9 short of 1000 aggregate runs himself for the calendar year.

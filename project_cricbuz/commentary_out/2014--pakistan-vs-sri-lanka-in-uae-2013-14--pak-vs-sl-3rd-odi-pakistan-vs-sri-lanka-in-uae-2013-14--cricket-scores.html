Shahid Afridi's form with the bat has been good too as the all-rounder has scored a couple of useful quick-fire 30s, batting in the death. Umar Akmal is the only batsman who has not spent time in the middle and Pakistan will be hoping that he will be ready if an opportunity arises for him. While he was expensive in the first game, he bowled a lot more economically in the second match. Saeed Ajmal and Hafeez have been keeping it tight while Afridi, after a good showing with the ball in the first ODI, went for a few in the last game.

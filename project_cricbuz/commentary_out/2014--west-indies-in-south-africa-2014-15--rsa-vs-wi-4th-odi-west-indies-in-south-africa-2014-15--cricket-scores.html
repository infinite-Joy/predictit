Their batsmen have failed and the bowlers have been reduced to being mere spectators in front of a rampaging opposition. Stats reveal mediocre numbers with only opener, Dwayne Smith and wicket-keeper batsman, Denesh Ramdin, coming close to the 100-run mark. Chris Gayle, who was impressive in the T20Is has been a major disappointment, having managed only 61 runs from his three visits to the middle. If the batsmen have failed, less said the better about the bowlers. With three wickets, Andre Russell, has been the pick, but his wickets have come at a price.

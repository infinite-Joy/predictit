Australia's batting was at its best in the opening match with Matthew Wade, David Hussey and Michael Hussey coming up with fine performances. However, the same cannot be said of Australia's efforts in the second game as the batsmen were not quite up to the mark. Only skipper Clarke showed some resolve with a hard-fought half-century as he tried to keep the batting unit together. What has been the feature of the Australian bowling is that that they have never given up and they always keep coming at the batsmen.

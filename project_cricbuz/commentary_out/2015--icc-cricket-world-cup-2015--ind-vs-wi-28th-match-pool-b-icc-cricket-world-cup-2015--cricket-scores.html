Why? Because no-one expected them to win and against one of the all-time great sides in history. Also before that win, cricket wasn't the crazy, popular and all-encompassing sport in the country that it is now. India - against all odds pulled off one of the greatest upsets in sporting history when they defended 183 against West Indies. It was a historic day in the annals of Indian cricket and 'Kapil's Devils' wrote their names into the record books. After that day, cricket in India was never the same and one can safely surmise that Indian cricket can be split into two halves.

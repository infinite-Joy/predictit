That method has left them looking amateurish and more importantly has placed them at the brink of a series defeat. West Indies now need to look at the way their opponents have patiently built partnerships and adopt a similar approach if they are to stage a comeback in this series. A lot of it has to do with the side's inability to build stable partnerships. In fact, the highest partnership for West Indies this series has been just 57 runs and it has come for the 9th wicket, between Sunil Narine and Ravi Rampaul. Those batting figures do not make for good reading and to add to West Indies' woes, the bowling hasn't been at its penetrative best. Ravi Rampaul was the standout bowler in the 2nd ODI after he took a five wicket haul and the West Indies will have their hopes pinned on him.

Sri Lanka's weak middle order has been the talking point for most of the summer and at Nottingham, England blew them away without much effort. Dilshan has not looked at his best at the top since his thumb injury. The two stalwarts of Sri Lankan cricket, Mahela Jayawardene and Kumar Sangakkara, have been the only consistent performers in this series. The Sri Lankan batsmen will have to work hard to counter the swinging ball.Their bowling was wayward at Trent Bridge, with none of the bowlers even threatening to trouble the openers.

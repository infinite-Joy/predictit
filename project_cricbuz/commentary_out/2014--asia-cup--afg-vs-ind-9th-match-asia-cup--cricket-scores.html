Afghanistan also have plenty of options in the spin department with the presence of Mohammad Nabi, Samiullah Shenwari and Hamza Hotak.It is the batting that has been Afghanistan's main worry. While they have a bunch of talented batters, they have not been able to get substantial contributions from them, barring Asghar Stanikzai and Samiullah Shenwari's superb performance against Bangladesh. The likes of Noor Ali Zadran and Nawroz Mangal got off to promising starts in a couple of games but have not been able to make it big.

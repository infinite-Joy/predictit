Unless injuries force him to, Dhoni is unlikely to tinker with the batting order too much. Yuvraj Singh has been slowly finding his feet back in the national team and this would present another opportunity for him to get ticking. It will also be interesting to see if Harbhajan Singh gets a look-in.

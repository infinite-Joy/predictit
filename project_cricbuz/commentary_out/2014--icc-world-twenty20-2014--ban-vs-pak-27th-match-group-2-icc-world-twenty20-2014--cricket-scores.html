Soon after an abysmal opening game against India, Mohammad Hafeez's men routed a strong Australia and left them shell-shocked. They have some of the most talented T20 players in the side and on their day, any one or two of them can turn a game on its head.Pakistan were perhaps nervous against India and it seemed like nerves got the better of them. But they made amends against Australia and ensured they were still in the tournament, having opened their account.Key Players: There are so many of them. Shahid Afridi needs no introduction, neither does skipper Hafeez. Saeed Ajmal is the best off-spinner in the world and he can make that ball turn on almost any surface - and the Dhaka pitches have assisted spinners, so Bangladesh had better watch out.On a side note, do not forget, the heavy assault, Ajmal came under against the same Bangladesh batsmen he will be up against Sunday.Ground realities: Bangladesh and Pakistan have played each other only once in the Shere Bangla Stadium in Mirpur, Dhaka, and the hosts ended up on the losing side.Stats:- Three Pakistan bowlers - Ajmal, Gul and Afridi feature in the top three list of wicket-takers in T20 internationals. While Ajmal has the most with 83, Gul has 77 and Afridi 75- Pakistan T20 captain Mohammad Hafeez has an impressive record in the shortest format.

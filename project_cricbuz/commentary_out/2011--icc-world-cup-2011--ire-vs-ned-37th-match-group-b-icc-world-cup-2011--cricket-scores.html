Amongst the minnows from both groups, Ireland are by far the best during this World Cup. Their biggest moment of the tournament came when they pulled off an upset win against England in a high-scoring encounter in Bangalore and the star was Kevin O' Brien, who hit the fastest hundred in all World Cups. Ireland could have had a couple of more wins, but fell agonizingly short of that in the games against Bangladesh and West Indies. Even in the matches against the tournament favourites such as South Africa and India, Ireland showed that had the sting in them to trouble the bigger sides. Doeschate also scored an unbeaten half-century against Bangladesh, despite the other players failing to provide him support.

South Africa suffered a heartbreak in the World Cup semifinal at the hands of the Kiwis and then endured a ODI series defeat in Bangladesh. Though the first fixture is being billed as the 'revenge' for the World Cup loss, De Villiers will be aware of the contrasting context of the first game of a bilateral ODI series and the World Cup semifinal his side bowed out of.

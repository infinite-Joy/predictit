But three days later, up against the class and guile of Sri Lanka's bowlers, the Dutch were blown away to bits.The same men, who had so heroically taken on the Irish bowlers, were in the eye of a storm against Nuwan Kulasekara, Angelo Mathews and Lasith Malinga, as the Europeans collapsed to 39 all out in 10.3 overs.The game against South Africa is by no means an easy proposition but at least that will provide them with a chance to fight them hard and redeem some lost pride. Even a semblance of competitiveness against South Africa could do Netherlands some good in the time to come.Key Players: The likes of Borren, Myburgh, Barresi and Cooper are more than capable batsmen in this format and they showed signs of their destruction capabilities in the run riot over Ireland, but they will need to pull up their socks against better teams, laced with better bowlers. How they come back against South Africa after their mauling against Sri Lanka is up to these key men, who will hold Dutch hopes with their bats.Ground reality:The game between South Africa and Netherlands will be the 21st match of this tournament and will be played at the Zahur Ahmed Chowdhury Stadium in Chitagong.
